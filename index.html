Characters of Game Of Thrones

Characters of Game Of Thrones

Jon Snow

He joins the Night’s Watch, following in the footsteps of his Uncle Benjen. His direwolf is named Ghost.

Robert Baratheon

King of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros and longtime friend of Ned Stark. Robert was engaged to Ned's sister, Lyanna, whose abduction by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen incited Robert's uprising against the throne.

Tyrion Lannister

Called "The Imp," because of his small stature, he is the youngest son of Tywin Lannister, the richest lord in the Seven Kingdoms and younger brother to Queen Cersei and Ser Jaime Lannister. What he lacks in height, he makes up in wit.

Cersei Lannister

Queen of the Seven Kingdoms and wife of King Robert. Fiercely protective of her three children, she also has an unusually tight connection to her twin brother, Jaime.

Daenerys Targaryen

Princess of House Targaryen, living in exile in Essos with her brother, Viserys.

Robb Stark

The oldest of Ned and Cat's five children. His direwolf is named Grey Wind.
